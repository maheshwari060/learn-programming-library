# 📜 Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful.
- Be kind and constructive.
- Welcome newcomers.
- Focus on learning and collaboration.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment
- Discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Spam

Let's build a positive learning community together.
